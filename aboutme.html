About me

👋 Heyyyy! So glad you are here

I’m Marcos Blanco, a former healthcare professional turned tech enthusiast, currently diving deep into the world of software development and game design. Originally from Brazil 🇧🇷, where I earned my Bachelor’s in Dentistry and a Master’s in Orthodontics 🦷, I’ve always enjoyed helping people through precision and creativity—qualities that I now bring to my work in technology.

Now based in Vancouver 🇨🇦, and pursuing a Computer Science 💻 degree at Langara College , I’m excited to grow my skills in programming, game development, and app creation. My portfolio showcases a few projects I’ve built to explore the possibilities of JavaScript, C#, Python and frameworks like React and Unity. I’m passionate about creating interactive, user-focused applications and games that offer unique and engaging experiences.

I approach this journey with humility, fully aware that I have a long path ahead. Every line of code teaches me something new, and I embrace each challenge as an opportunity to grow. While there’s much to learn, I’m committed to pushing forward, honing my skills, and contributing wherever I can. I know that technology is where I belong, and I’m excited for the road ahead—one project, one lesson, one breakthrough at a time.

When I’m not coding, you’ll probably find me gaming, working out, or exploring new places. I believe in the power of learning, adaptability, and combining creativity with code to build meaningful projects. Thank you for visiting my portfolio—I'm excited to connect and collaborate on future projects!
